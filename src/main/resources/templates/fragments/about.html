My Background

Developer by Profession, Problem Solver by Passion.

I'm Nithin Burugu — a dedicated software developer with a strong foundation in building full-stack applications using Java, Spring Boot, ReactJS, and MySQL. I graduated with a B.Tech in Information Technology from B V Raju Institute of Technology, where I developed a keen interest in crafting scalable and efficient backend systems. My experience spans developing RESTful APIs, containerized microservices with Docker, and creating modern UI experiences that bridge functionality with design.

During my internship at miniOrange, I worked on enterprise-level applications such as an IdP-initiated SAML SSO app for Atlassian Crowd and contributed to plugins like the Crowd OAuth and PDF Exporter add-ons. I gained hands-on experience in authentication protocols like SAML 2.0 and OAuth 2.0, backend logic implementation, and cloud app development on Atlassian Forge. These experiences strengthened my ability to build secure, scalable, and production-ready systems.

Beyond development, I’m passionate about problem-solving and continuous learning — having solved over 1000+ coding challenges across platforms like LeetCode, CodeChef, and GeeksforGeeks, and ranking among the top 10% globally. My journey reflects a commitment to writing clean, maintainable code and a drive to grow as both a developer and mentor. I’m always eager to collaborate, innovate, and contribute to impactful projects that push the boundaries of technology.
